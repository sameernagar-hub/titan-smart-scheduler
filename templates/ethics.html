How To Read This

The ethics record is informative rather than absolute.

Strong alignment means the plan’s metrics and constraints strongly support that theory.
Conditional alignment means the plan generally supports the theory but still needs context or human review.
Needs review means conflicts, overload, weak coverage, or missing context make the ethical case harder to defend.
